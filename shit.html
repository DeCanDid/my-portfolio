About Easy Bistro

Classic Bistro, Raw Bar, & Seafood Restaurants in Chattanooga, TN

Easy Bistro & Bar is one of the most well-regarded restaurants in Chattanooga, TN. Since opening in 2005 by husband-and-wife team Erik and Amanda Niel, Easy has evolved continually, and in May 2020, the restaurant reopened in a brand new space (with a reimagined menu!) in Chattanooga’s West Village neighborhood. Chef Erik Niel (a two-time James Beard Award nominee for Best Chef: Southeast) created a menu inspired by his childhood in Louisiana and his training in classic French fare while celebrating local flavor and ingredients from the Tennessee River Valley. At Easy, we’re constantly pushing ourselves to find new ways to redefine the dining experience, and we’ve embraced a curated selection of small and large plates meant to be shared – highlighting classic bistro dishes, fresh seafood, wood-burning oven dishes, house-made pasta, and a full raw bar (featuring oysters from the nearby Gulf coast as well as East and West coast varieties). Easy Bistro also has a nationally recognized wine and craft cocktail program that features rotating seasonal cocktails and an extensive bourbon and whiskey list. Come see us!

About Us

CuisineShare til it Hurts


At Easy, we curate experiences based on how we like to dine – so our menu is meant to be shared, offering a family-style dining experience. We have let go of coursed dining and are not looking back.

In an effort to encourage this new style of dining for Chattanoogans, the menu we’ve created is elemental at its core, mirroring the way you’d entertain guests in your home. Guests can try a whole slew of menu items with friends or family with the help of our staff, who can curate endless options for dish combinations. Our menu items honor simplicity yet never lack creativity or flavor. We’re pushing the boundaries and are excited to share that with you.

The Bar at Easy Bistro

BECAUSE INCREDIBLE FOOD DESERVES A COMPANION

Join us at the bar to celebrate a special occasion, relax after a long day, or simply enjoy a nightcap.

Easy Bistro’s bar program is robust, to say the least. We believe beverages are an important part of the dining experience, so we put a lot of time and effort into creating a cocktail menu that’s worthy of the food. Our wine list is curated to complement our cuisine and features a variety of both new and old-world producers with style. As whiskey and bourbon enthusiasts, we pride ourselves on our selection of more than 300 whiskies – including Scotch, Irish, and even Japanese and Taiwanese whiskies – each with the power to elevate your meal.

Raw Barat Easy Bistro

CHATTANOOGA’S RAW OYSTER BAR

We love oysters. Our selection of fresh, farm-raised varieties changes daily, featuring oysters from the nearby Golf coast, as well as some of the best East and West coast varieties.

Easy Bistro’s raw bar has always been central to the menu and greets guests as soon as they walk in the door. With a focus on the freshest and highest-quality oysters, Easy features oysters from the nearby Gulf coast, as well as some of the best East and West coast varieties. Guests are often amazed at the difference and uniqueness of flavor accounted for by region. Also in the raw bar is a wood-fired hearth used to prepare dishes such as hearth-roasted oysters, whole fish, and octopus.

Enjoy your next gathering in our private dining space– chic, intimate, and customizable to suit your taste.
